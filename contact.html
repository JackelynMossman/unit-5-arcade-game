I am up for anything!

I am open to good and bad feedback (just don't be rude). I am doing this because a good portion of the world's inhabitants travel....even the birds.

From the moment we wake up to the moment we put our jammas back on...we consider the possibilities. By nature, we are curious...some people more than others. The point is this my friends...we often ask each other..."Have you travelled here before?" The answer might possibly be no... So I am documenting my consumption of the world in the hopes to help people make informed spending decisions when it comes to travel. No wasting time or money on travel flops.

Time is not a renewable resource, so let's be sure we can say #ItWasWorthIt. I will do my best to be open minded and try to be non-biased in my opinions. AND most importantly...honest.

I believe in karma and I like to make sure that I go to bed at night with my good karma reserves topped up to the brim.

Thank you for listening and be kind to each other and the space around you!
