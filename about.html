Who is this woman?

Jackelyn aka Jacko or Jack

(but never ever Jackie)

I am just a girl with an untamed spirit, an opinion larger than life and an unapologetic cry baby when I watch the TV series "This Is Us".

I am a mother of a 20 year old daughter whoâ€¦much to her chagrin did not fall far from this tree. I am a mother of a recently passed away 10 year Golden Retriever who loved unconditionally, was as soft as a pillow, and could out fart and burp any person I know.

I am a beer snob...Craft not Crap (A little game I made up and am happy to share the rules). I smoke cigars, love white chocolate, hate the rain, am made of cheese and will accept any cooking challenge put forth to me. Bring it!

I have traveled my whole life, most often solo, since I was in grade 8. My parents were very trusting, I stayed in Mexico with a couple girlfriends, no parents, for a month in grade 9. I lived in Japan and went to school in grade 10 & 11 and also worked there after I graduated...so much experience before I turned 19.

I was lucky, very lucky to have had such amazing parents who offered up life altering experiences on a silver platter. They worked their asses off to give my sister and I everything that was needed to become happy well rounded humans. They are, by the way, still happily married. 46 years later, they still date and go parking. My dad still makes her giggle and blush.

My dad = best guy on earth
