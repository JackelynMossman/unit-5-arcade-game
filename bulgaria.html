Mysterious

Sofia, Bulgaria

3 Cities...big and small

Bulgaria left me with a roller coaster of feelings and thoughts. The country and the people are beautiful through and through, there is no doubt about that.

I knew before I even left Canada that I would never forget her (Bulgaria = her). We started in a super rad town called Veliko Tarnovo and it was LOVE at first sight.

After a crazy crazy bus ride we arrived in Plovdiv, ALIVE....but not for a lack of trying by the bus driver. Again, I felt a kindred spirit connection here from the food, to the Rakia, to the fashion and...oh yes, more food.

Then came Sofia, the city I looked most forward to experiencing and she left me empty. From the moment we arrived at the mediocre Airbnb to the tragic food to the strange sight at Alexander Nevsky Cathedral, it was a collection of unfortunate events that left us slighted.
